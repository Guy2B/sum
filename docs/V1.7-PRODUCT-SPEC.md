# Σ V1.7 — Product specification

## Positioning

Σ is not a replacement for Gmail, social networks, accounting software or a medical application. It is a decision layer that normalises authorised signals and proposes the next useful action.

Primary launch audience: the person responsible for an activity of one to five people — independent professional, consultant, coach, creator or micro-business owner.

## Commercial offer

- **Σ Free**: one active workspace, local essentials, export, limited guided analysis.
- **Σ Pro monthly**: 8.90 EUR per month.
- **Σ Pro annual**: 69 EUR per year.
- Pro includes one chosen active workspace, not five simultaneous workspaces. The user may change workspace without deleting data.

## Main navigation

1. **Today** — up to three justified recommendations.
2. **Attention** — unified queue for communication, opportunities, admin, execution and capacity.
3. **Plan** — daily capacity, workload, three protected outcomes and suggested order.
4. **Σ Coach** — grounded conversation using allowed workspace signals.
5. **Sources** — permissions, connection state, synchronisation, deletion and disconnection.

Detailed modules remain available under More/Tools, but they do not dominate the home screen.

## Context graph

The V1.7 intelligence layer links:

- objective and declared constraints;
- task, project and event;
- sender/client and message;
- social interaction and source publication;
- transaction and administrative obligation;
- sleep/activity/energy signal;
- learning resource and objective;
- recommendation, user action and feedback.

The graph is lightweight and local in the admin beta. It is used for ranking and explanation, not for automated irreversible decisions.

## Advice rules

- show at most three primary recommendations;
- explain the signals used and a confidence score;
- never invent deadlines, amounts, health states or provider access;
- ask a question only when confidence is low or two important outcomes conflict;
- require confirmation before sending, publishing, deleting or modifying external content;
- treat health, legal, tax and regulated financial subjects as organisational guidance only.

## Data sources

### Communication

Gmail, Microsoft 365/Outlook, Yahoo and GMX feed a unified mail queue. Social email notifications are complementary signals and are deduplicated against direct social API records.

### Direct social adapters

Instagram Business/Creator, Facebook Pages, YouTube, X, LinkedIn and TikTok have direct adapter paths. Actual fields depend on scopes, account type, commercial API plan and provider approval. The interface always reports connected, partial, approval-required or unavailable status.

### Health

The mobile bridge contract supports Apple Health/Apple Watch, Android Health Connect and Samsung Health/Galaxy Watch. Only authorised daily summary signals are used for workload guidance.

## AI architecture

1. deterministic calculations and policy rules;
2. optional multilingual semantic model in the browser using Transformers.js and WASM/WebGPU;
3. optional browser-native generative model where available;
4. optional self-hosted local Ollama gateway for cross-browser rewriting.

A generative model may rewrite a deterministic answer but must preserve its facts, numbers and uncertainty.

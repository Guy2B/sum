# Σ Context Center — V1.6.1

Connected platforms provide events and messages, but they do not explain what the user is trying to protect. The Context Center captures the missing decision layer:

- primary and secondary goals;
- visible definition of progress;
- available time and focus capacity;
- energy preference and active days;
- fixed commitments and current pressure;
- constraints that Σ must respect;
- preferred tone and answer depth;
- explicit permission to combine selected modules;
- weekly check-ins containing wins, blockers and next focus.

The Dashboard displays a context-completeness score. The Coach includes these fields in its verified context and respects disabled domains during cross-analysis.
